WHAT'S THE CONTEST ABOUT?

It's been a year since we released the first issue of She Shreds, and we've got big plans for the future. She Shreds will be launching a major fundraising campaign in December to help us expand internationally, and we want to include YOU, our awesome readers and supporters, in that campaign.

WHAT DO YOU WIN?

We're excited to see all your videos! To show our gratitude, we'll enter everyone who submits a video into a drawing for all three issues of She Shreds Magazine autographed by the bands/artists on the cover--Corin Tucker, Marnie Stern, and La Luz.

WHAT ARE THE RULES

CLICK HERE TO READ THE FINE PRINT
  • Your video can be as short or long as you like
  • Try to make sure you're in a well-lit environment and that your audio is loud and clear.
  • Please don't include any copyrighted materials, like other bands' songs or clothes with company logos, in your video! That makes things tricky for us.
  • The higher quality your video is, the better! Don't worry about editing your video; original footage is welcome.
  • Submit your video by uploading it to a filesharing site (Dropbox, Google Drive, WeTransfer, etc.) and e-mail it to us at editorial@sheshredsmag.com with the download link, along with your name, age, and city/country you live in. You can also use the form below!
